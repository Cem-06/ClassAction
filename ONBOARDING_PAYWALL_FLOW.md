
# ONBOARDING_PAYWALL_FLOW.md
Lawsy — Onboarding & Paywall Flow Specification

Purpose:
Design a high‑conversion onboarding flow that demonstrates value quickly, creates a strong “money opportunity” perception, and leads users into a subscription paywall.

Core Psychological Triggers
- Money visualization
- Social proof
- FOMO (fear of missing out)
- Personalization
- Instant perceived value

Target Outcome:
Users feel that they might be missing out on money and want to unlock the settlements they qualify for.

---

# Overview of Flow

Total Screens: 7

1. Hook Screen
2. Big Numbers Screen
3. Social Proof Screen
4. Personalization Screen
5. Eligibility Scan Screen
6. Potential Results Screen
7. Paywall Screen

---
